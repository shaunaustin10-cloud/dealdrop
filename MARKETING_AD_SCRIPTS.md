## Technical Setup for Ads

### 1. The Creative (Video)
*   **Format:** 9:16 Vertical Video (1080x1920).
*   **Captions:** MUST have captions (many people watch on mute).
*   **Music:** Use trending audio for organic; use "Royalty Free" or Commercial library for Paid Ads.

### 2. The Funnel
*   **Destination:** Send traffic to your Register Page (`/register`).
*   **Offer:** Ensure the "Free Tier" is prominent. Low friction.

### 3. Targeting (If paying)
*   **Interests:** "Real Estate Investing", "BiggerPockets", "Wholesaling", "Gary Keller", "Grant Cardone".
*   **Demographics:** Ages 25-45 (most active tech adopters in REI).
