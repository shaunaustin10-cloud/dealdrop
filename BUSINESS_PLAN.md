# Business Plan: REI Deal Drop

**Date:** February 9, 2026
**Version:** 2.0
**Status:** Live Marketplace Launch

---

## 1. Executive Summary
**REI Deal Drop** is an AI-powered real estate investment platform that combines a rigorous underwriting engine with a **curated, off-market marketplace**. We don't just list deals; we vet them. By leveraging Generative AI (Gemini 2.0 Flash) to act as a "conservative veteran underwriter," we bring transparency to a notoriously opaque industry.

**Core Value Proposition:**
*   **For Buyers:** "First Look" access to pre-vetted, high-score deals. No more digging through trash.
*   **For Wholesalers:** Instant credibility. Instead of a shady text message, you send a professional, AI-verified deal packet.
*   **For Agents:** A "CMA on Steroids" that wins listings by showing sellers data-backed visuals.

---

## 2. Company Overview

### 2.1 Mission Statement
To create the most trusted marketplace for off-market real estate, where every deal is backed by transparent data and rigorous analysis.

### 2.2 The Problem
*   **The "Wild West" Marketplace:** Off-market deals are scattered across Facebook groups, text blasts, and email chains. Fraud is rampant.
*   **Trust Deficit:** Buyers don't trust wholesaler numbers (ARV/Rehab).
*   **Speed vs. Accuracy:** Investors lose good deals because manual due diligence takes too long.

### 2.3 The Solution
A **hybrid SaaS + Marketplace** platform.
1.  **SaaS:** AI tools for analyzing any property.
2.  **Marketplace:** A "Live" feed of deals that have passed our scoring criteria. VIP members get "First Look" access before the general public.

## 3. Market Analysis

### 3.1 Target Audience
| Persona | Pain Point | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **VIP Buyers (Pros)** | Access to deal flow is competitive. | **"First Look"** access to deals scoring 84+; exclusive inventory. |
| **Wholesalers (Sellers)** | Buyers ignore their emails; low credibility. | **"Verified"** badge; ability to post to a qualified buyer pool. |
| **Agents** | Need to differentiate in listing appointments. | Branded, professional PDF reports that justify pricing. |

### 3.2 Market Trends
*   **"Flight to Quality":** In a tighter economy, investors are pickier. "Trash" deals don't sell. Our "Deal Score" filters the noise.
*   **Tech-Enabled Wholesaling:** The industry is moving away from "bandit signs" to digital marketplaces.

## 4. Product & Services

### 4.1 The Live Marketplace (New!)
*   **Curated Inventory:** Not just a dump of addresses. Deals are scored.
*   **VIP First Look:** High-scoring deals (84+) are locked for Pro members for the first 48 hours.
*   **Map-Based Discovery:** Visual search using Google Maps integration.
*   **Verification:** "Verified Contract" badges for wholesalers who upload proof of equitable interest.

### 4.2 AI Underwriter Engine
*   **Conservative Grading:** Our AI (Gemini) looks for "fluff" and underestimates.
*   **Deal Score (0-100):** A single metric to sort good deals from bad.
*   **RentCast Integration:** Real-time rental comps and market value data.

### 4.3 Reporting
*   **PDF Packets:** One-click generation of marketing assets for sellers.

## 6. Marketing & Sales Strategy

### 6.1 Go-to-Market: "The Marketplace Launch"
1.  **The "Seeded" Launch:**
    *   Partner with 3-5 high-volume wholesalers in key markets (e.g., Indianapolis, Florida).
    *   They agree to post their best deals exclusively on Deal Drop for 48 hours.
    *   We drive our buyer list to them.

2.  **Viral "Deal Drops":**
    *   **"Friday Drops":** Release a batch of new, high-score deals every Friday at noon. Build anticipation.
    *   **Social Proof:** "Just Sold" case studies showing the spread earned by the buyer.

3.  **Content Marketing:**
    *   **"Deal of the Week" Breakdowns:** Video teardowns of the best deal on the marketplace.
    *   **"The Roast":** Users submit deals for us to analyze/roast on social media.

## 7. Operational Plan

### 7.1 Technology Stack
*   **Frontend:** React, Tailwind, Vite.
*   **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Functions).
*   **AI:** Gemini 2.0 Flash.
*   **Payments:** Stripe.

### 7.2 Key Metrics (KPIs)
*   **GMV (Gross Merchandise Value):** Total value of property sold through the platform.
*   **Unlock Rate:** % of users who click to "Unlock" a deal (view address).
*   **Pro Conversion:** % of free users who upgrade to see a locked "First Look" deal.

## 8. Financial Roadmap

### Phase 1: Marketplace Seeding (Months 1-3)
*   **Goal:** 50 Active Listings, 200 Registered Buyers.
*   **Focus:** Manual recruiting of sellers. Concierge onboarding.
*   **Revenue:** Focus on subscription growth via "First Look" value.

### Phase 2: Liquidity (Months 4-9)
*   **Goal:** Deals selling within 7 days.
*   **Focus:** Automating the "Post Deal" flow. Marketing automation.
*   **Revenue Target:** $10k MRR.

### Phase 3: Transactional (Months 10+)
*   **Goal:** Capture transaction fees (e.g., deposit handling).
*   **Focus:** Integrated payments for EMD (Earnest Money Deposit).
